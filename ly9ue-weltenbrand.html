Weltenbrand

Hi, about a year ago i published my first sci-fi novel, Weltenbrand, and I recently published part two of the series. Looking over the posts in this subreddit, I thought there might be some that would be interested in it. Weltenbrand is an attempt to put a real hard sci-fi spin on the common space opera/military sci-fi novel, while keeping the best parts of them alive. There are still plenty of battles in space and on the ground, political plotting and backstabbing, espionage and personal. I got hold of this CD back in the day through mail order. We had stores in NYC like Generation Records that had a pretty good selection but for the obscure and the imports, Isolation Tank (now an online store known as IsoTank) and Digital Underground were the big ones. I can't remember which one this came from. The catalogs featured short blurbs describing the bands sound so you had a good 2-6 sentences to convince you whether or not you'd like them. It was a crapshoot!
